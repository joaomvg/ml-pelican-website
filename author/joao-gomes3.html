"VC dimension"

 Thu 30 April 2020  —  Joao Gomes

The VC dimension is a very important concept in machine learning theory. It gives a measure of complexity based on combinatorial aspects. This concept is used to show how certain infinite hypothesis classes are PAC-learnable. Some of the main concepts are explained: growth function and shattering. I give examples and show how the VC dimension is used to bound the generalisation error.
